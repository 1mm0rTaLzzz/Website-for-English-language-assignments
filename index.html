About us

Funny Larvas - a Russian-speaking semi-professional esports team for starcraft 2. Our players perform with the FLarva tag at single and team tournaments and show good results.

The name of the team honors the most important and at the same time undervalued Zerg units - larvae. Without larvae, zergs could not exist, because they are responsible for the construction of buildings and the creation of new units.
